Controls
W / A / S / D — Move your character

F — Use your currently equipped item (attack with weapons or heal with consumables)

E — Interact with the merchant (buy or sell items)

P — Pick up items in your current location

Arrow Up / Arrow Down — Move forward or backward between dungeon locations

Actions are real-time, and smart use of movement and timing is key to surviving.

How to Win
To win the game, you must defeat all enemies in the last location of the dungeon. Progress through each area and prepare for a final fight.

How You Can Lose
You lose the game if your health drops below 0. Use healing items wisely and avoid reckless fighting.

Enemy Types (by Color)
Red Enemies
Stand still and only attack once every 5 seconds if you come close.

Purple Enemies
Chase you directly and attack with melee. They are fast and aggressive.

Pink Enemies
Run away from you and attack from a distance. Their attacks are stronger than others.

Game Interface (Top of the Screen)
At the top of the screen, you’ll see:

Health Points — Your current health

Coins — Your gold amount

Inventory — The items you are carrying
